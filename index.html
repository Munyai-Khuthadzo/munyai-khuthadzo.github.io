About Me

Technical SEO Meets Data Engineering

I am a Technical SEO Specialist based in Limpopo, South Africa, with a unique combination of SEO expertise and SQL data engineering skills. While most SEO candidates check pages manually, I build automated data pipelines that find issues across thousands of URLs in seconds.

My approach combines Screaming Frog crawl data with MySQL queries, Google Search Console analysis, AI-assisted strategy, and Looker Studio dashboards — giving clients a complete, data-driven picture of their organic search performance.

I have applied this workflow to real South African websites, uncovering issues that manual audits miss entirely — including WWW vs non-WWW canonical mismatches, WordPress system file crawl waste, and internal linking structural problems.

"Combining SQL, SEO, and AI allows me to go beyond traditional keyword analysis and work directly with data to uncover deeper insights."
